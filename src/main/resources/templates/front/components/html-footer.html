About us

REVE Systems started in 2003 with a focused approach to serve the IP based communication industry. A Telecommunication & Software Solution provider, REVE Systems has a wide assortment of products, ranging from backbone infrastructure to peripheral products, including middleware. The company today holds a leadership position in Mobile VoIP, SIP Softswitch, VoIP Billing, Bandwidth Optimization, WebRTC, Enterprise Communication, e-Governance and Mobile OTT.

Headquartered in Singapore, REVE Systems has its major development centers in Bangladesh & India with regional presence in USA, UK, Russia, Lebanon and Hong Kong. The company serves customers in over 78 countries
